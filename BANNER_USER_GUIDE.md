## Hiding a Banner

### Method 1: From the Page
When you see a banner:
1. Look for the **close button** (X) or **dismiss option**
2. Click it
3. Banner disappears
4. It won't show again (until you reset it)

### Method 2: From Preferences
1. Go to Banner Preferences
2. Find the banner in "Visible Banners"
3. Click **"Masquer"** (Hide)
4. Banner moves to hidden list

---

## Showing a Hidden Banner Again

1. Go to Banner Preferences
2. Look at "Hidden Banners" section
3. Find the banner you want to see
4. Click **"Afficher"** (Show)
5. Banner will appear again

## FAQ

**Q: Will my preferences be saved?**
A: Yes! If you're logged in, your preferences are saved to your account and work across all your devices. If you're not logged in, they're saved locally (on that device only).

**Q: Can I get back a banner I hid?**
A: Yes! Go to your preferences and click "Show" next to any hidden banner.

**Q: What happens if I reset all banners?**
A: All the banners you've hidden will show again. You can hide them individually again anytime.

**Q: Do admins know when I hide banners?**
A: Yes, admins can see which users have hidden each banner to improve the platform. But they respect your choices - you can always show/hide as you wish.

**Q: Can I completely disable all banners?**
A: Not completely, but you can hide each one individually. Some important system banners may reappear if they're critical.

**Q: What if a banner is important but I keep hiding it?**
A: Admins monitor this and will adjust based on user feedback. Important banners may be repositioned or redesigned.

**Q: Do my preferences follow me across devices?**
A: Only if you're logged in! Each device has its own preferences if you browse anonymously.

## Notifications

Banners are different from notifications:
- **Banners** = Visual notices on the page
- **Notifications** = Messages/alerts (usually in a menu)
- **Emails** = Messages sent to your inbox

You manage banner preferences here. For email or other notifications, check your account settings.
